Anne Dickerson, PhD, OTR/L, SCDCM, FAOTA, FGSA is a professor in the Department of Occupational Therapy, East Carolina University. She is Director of the Research for the Older Adult Driver Initiative (ROADI) and current editor of Occupational Therapy in Health Care. Dr. Dickerson is an international leader in occupational therapy research in areas of older adults, driver simulation, and drivers with autism spectrum disorder, and driver rehabilitation.
Heather Panczykowski, DHSc, OTR/L, FAOTA is an associate professor in the Department of Occupational Therapy at East Carolina University. She has extensive experience working with children and youth and continues to address their needs using complementary approaches in her research. Dr. Panczykowski holds multiple certifications that enhance her ability to create impactful programs tailored to the needs of children and young adults with special needs.

Her dedication to individuals with disabilities was evident as she worked with Dr. Dickerson on these checklists ad a graduate assistant. Her OTD capstone project will be to enhance the community mobility checklists through multi-media education.
